## Tips

- **PDFs:** the mini-pages are already designed for print. Open any `/p/*.html` page in Chrome, hit Cmd/Ctrl+P, "Save as PDF" — you'll get a polished one-pager you can attach to emails or send to recruiters.
- **Hashtags:** the LinkedIn posts use `#AgenticAI` and `#RAG` consistently. Add company-specific or industry tags as relevant.
- **Cadence:** one LinkedIn post per week for six weeks lets each principle land on its own. Don't bundle all six in one post — the whole point of the per-principle pages is that each one is a separate shareable artifact.
- **Edits:** Read each post once before pasting. The voice is the showcase's voice; if you want it more conversational or more formal, adjust the opening line.
